BREAKING NEWS: Peckterial Infection Eradicates Chicken Population

Recently, chicken populations have been decreasing rapidly, leading to a major shortage of chicken products, including chicken nuggets. Subsequently, the global economy has taken a large hit. Leading economists predict this stock market crash to be “far worse than the Wall Street Crash of 1929 and the Stock Market Crash of 1987 combined.”

In early September of 2020, a bacteria known as Voronacirus eradicated the chicken population. Voronacirus' origin is still unknown. This highly infectious bacteria has many vectors of transmission, including airborne and seaborne transmission. The world is now in chaos, with everyone panicking that they won’t be able to eat chicken nuggets ever again.
