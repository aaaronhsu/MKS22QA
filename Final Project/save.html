Save the World

Over the past couple years, life has not treated you well. You were kicked out of your mom’s basement because you stole your mom’s credit card and spent all of her money on Fortnite, with dreams of being an epic gamer one day.

You pace around the streets, your new home, thinking of ways to save yourself. With the chicken pandemic sweeping the world, everyone is craving chicken nuggets. You decide to open up your own restaurant to serve this growing demand for chicken nuggets with your very own nugget recipe. Save both yourself and the world right?

You open your restaurant and realize that you don’t know how to make any type of nugget. You consult the internet and they give you a couple recommendations. It’s your job to craft the perfect nugget recipe to satisfy the growing demand for nuggets.
